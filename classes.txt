Class: Boat

Attributes:
boat_name (string)
material (array)
crew (array)
dock_location (string)
is_docked (boolean)
weight (float)
destination (string)
number_of_passengers (integer)

Methods:
change_name (changes the boat_name)
add_crew_member (adds "(crew member name)" to the crew array)
ship_out (changes the is_docked boolean)
add_passenger (changes the number_of_passengers)
change_destination (changes the destination string)
repair_boat (may add to the material array)
