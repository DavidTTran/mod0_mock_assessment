Doing Laundry

1. take off dirty clothes
2. put dirty clothes in hamper
3. get dirty clothes
  3.1 check hamper
  3.2 check room
    3.2.1 check underneath the bed
    3.2.2 remove bed sheets
    3.2.3 check bathroom
      3.2.3.1 get towels
  3.3 check garage
    3.3.1 check car
4. sort dirty clothes
  4.1 lights
  4.2 darks
  4.3 washer safe
  4.4 steam clean
    4.4.1 set aside for later
5. open washer
6. load dirty clothes into washer
7. add detergent
  7.1 if light clothes
    7.1.1 can add bleach
8. close washer door
9. turn on washer
10. set washer
  10.1 check settings
    10.1.1 time
    10.1.2 temperature
    10.1.3 spin
11. begin cycle
12. wait
  12.1 make a sandwich
13. open washer
14. unload washer
15. open dryer
16. load into dryer
17. close dryer
18. check settings
  18.1 time
  18.2 temperature
  18.3 dryness
19. begin cycle
20. wait
  20.1 make another sandwich
21. open dryer
22. unload dryer
23. separate clothes
24. fold clothes
  24.1 except for wrap around bedsheets because who knows how to fold those
